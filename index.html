For science!

This artifact is built for a master thesis in information system during spring 2016 by John Fahlgren. The purpose is to collect tweets related to antibiotics and antibiotic use in a larger scale. Hopefully the data, when analysed, could help to understand some parts of the ongoing issue of antibiotic resistance in a better way.
